How to Get Started

Icebreaker

Icebreaker gatherings are an informal way to meet people and find out more about Fuquay-Varina United Methodist Church. They're a warm, welcoming introduction to the church, pastors, staff members and leaders of our ministries.

For details about the next monthly gathering, contact info@fvumc.org or call the office at 919-552-4331.

conneXions

Interested in exploring membership at Fuquay-Varina United Methodist Church? ConneXions is for you. The class is open to anyone, whether you're new to the church, new to the faith, or new to United Methodism. It's a two-Sunday class led by our pastors and staff where we share what it means to be a member of Fuquay-Varina United Methodist Church.

For details about the next class, contact info@fvumc.org or call the office at 919-552-4331.

Children and Youth

This community of faith has vibrant ministries for children and youth including small group settings, social activites, mission projects, music programs, scouting groups, and Sunday School. We want to be sure young people grow up knowing that God created them, God loves them, and God wants to be friends with them. Our programs help young people learn about God through the Bible and what these teachings mean for us today. We also offer opportunities for children and youth to live out their faith in God through meaningful service.

For more information about children's programs (birth – 5th grade), contact Emily Sterling, Director of Children's Ministries, at esterling@fvumc.org esterling@fvumc.org.

For information about youth programs, (6th – 12th grades), contact Pastor Kevin Johnson at kjohnson@fvumc.org.
