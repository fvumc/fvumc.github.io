conneXions

If you wish to explore membership at Fuquay Varina United Methodist Church, ConneXions is for you. The class is for anyone interested in membership, whether new to the church, new to the faith, or new to Methodism. It's a two-Sunday class led by our pastors and staff where we share what it means to be a member of Fuquay Varina United Methodist Church. For details about the next class, contact XXXX

Kids

Our vibrant children's ministries teaches children that God created them, God loves them, and God wants to be friends with them. We want our children to accept God’s love for them and for children to love God in return. We teach our children to learn about God through the Bible and what Bible stories mean for us today. We give our children opportunities to live out their faith in God through meaningful service. For more information about our children's programs, contact the director of children's ministries Emily Sterling at esterling@fvumc.org
